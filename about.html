Our Story

Fresh Bake Bakery opened in 2020 with one simple dream — to become the go-to neighbourhood bakery in Hatfield, Pretoria. We bake everything fresh daily using the best local ingredients.

Vision: To be the neighbourhood bakery of choice by offering great food, creative ideas, and excellent customer service.
